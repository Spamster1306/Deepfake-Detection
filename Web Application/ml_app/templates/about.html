DEEP FAKE DETECTION

  • Deepfake detection is a critical technology aimed at identifying and mitigating the rising threat of digitally manipulated media.
  • By employing advanced algorithms and machine learning techniques, deepfake detection systems analyze videos and images for inconsistencies, artifacts, and unnatural patterns that indicate tampering.
  • These systems often focus on facial and voice recognition, scrutinizing minute details such as blinking patterns, lip movements, and vocal intonations to distinguish real footage from synthetic creations.
